h2. Avocado Egg Salad

* 1 medium avocado
* 1 small lime, juiced
* 1 small jalapeño, finely diced
* 2 tablespoon cilantro, chopped
* 2 green onions, sliced
* 4 hard boiled eggs, peeled and diced
* 1 tablespoon mayonnaise (optional)
* 6 strips bacon, chopped (optional)
* pink peppercorn (optional)

# Mash avocado in a mixing bowl. Add lime juice, jalapeño, cilantro, onions, and mix.
# Fold in eggs. Add more lime juice or mayonaise for a moister texture.
# Optionally, add bacon, peppercorn and serve on toast or a sandwich.
