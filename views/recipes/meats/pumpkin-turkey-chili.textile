h2. Pumpkin Turkey Chili

* 1 Tbsp oil
* 1 onion, diced
* 1 bell pepper, diced
* 3 cloves garlic, chopped
* 1 tsp cumin
* ~1 lbs ground turkey
* 1 (15 oz) can white beans, rinsed and drained
* 1 (15 oz) can diced tomatoes
* 1 (15 oz) can pumpkin pureé
* 2 cups vegetable stock or pumpkin ale
* 1 tsp oregano
* 1/4 tsp chipotle chile powder (optional)
* 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
* 1/4 tsp ground ginger
* 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
* 1/8 tsp ground cloves
* salt and pepper to taste

# Heat the oil in a pan over medium heat, add the onion, cook until tender. Add the bell pepper, garlic and cumin. Cook until tender.
# Add the turkey and cook until browned, about 15 minutes.
# Add the beans, tomatoes, pumpkin pureé, broth, and remaining spices. Bring to a boil. Reduce the heat and simmer for 25 minutes.
