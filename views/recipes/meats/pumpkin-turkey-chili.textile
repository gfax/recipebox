h2. Pumpkin Turkey Chili
* 1 Tbsp oil
* 1 onion, diced
* 1 bell pepper, diced
* 3 cloves garlic, chopped
* 1 tsp cumin
* ~1 lbs ground turkey
* 1 (15 oz) can white beans, rinsed and drained
* 1 (15 oz) can diced tomatoes
* 2 cups pumpkin puree or 1 (15 ounce) can pumpkin puree
* 2 cups chicken broth or turkey stock or pumpkin ale
* 1 tsp oregano
* 1 tsp pumpkin pie spice
* 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
* 1/4 tsp ground ginger
* 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
* 1/4 tsp ground cloves
* salt and pepper to taste

# Heat the oil in a pan over medium heat, add the onion, cook for 5 minutes, add the bell pepper and cook until the onion is tender, about 3-5 minutes.
Add the garlic and cumin and cook until fragrant, about a minute.
# Add the ground beef and cook until browned, about 15 minutes.
# Add the beans, tomatoes, pumpkin puree, broth, oregano, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, cloves, bring to a boil, reduce the heat and simmer for 25 minutes
