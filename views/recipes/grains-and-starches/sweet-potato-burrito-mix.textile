h2. Sweet Potato Burrito Mix

* 1 Tbsp vegetable oil
* 1 onion, chopped
* 4 cloves garlic, minced
* 45 oz kidney beans, rinsed, drained, partially mashed
* 4 cups sweet potato, cooked and mashed
* 2 cups water
* 3 Tbsp chili powder
* 2 tsp cumin
* 4 tsp mustard
* 1/4 tsp chipotle chile powder, or to taste
* 3 Tbsp soy sauce

# Stir in beans and sweet potato. Gradually stir in water, until mixture reaches desired consistency.
