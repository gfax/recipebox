h2. Sweet Potato Burrito Mix

* 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
* 1 onion, chopped
* 4 cloves garlic, minced
* 6 cups kidney beans, rinsed and drained
* 2 cups water
* 3 Tbsp chili powder
* 2 tsp ground cumin
* 4 tsp prepared mustard
* 1/4 tsp chipotle chile powder, or to taste
* 3 Tbsp soy sauce
* 4 cups cooked and mashed sweet potatoes

# Preheat oven to 350F/175C.
# Heat oil in a medium skillet, and saute onion and garlic until soft.
# Stir in beans, and mash. Gradually stir in water, and heat until warm.
# Stir in the chili powder, cumin, mustard, cayenne pepper and soy sauce.
