h2. Chocolate Granola

* 1 (18 oz) can of oats
* 1/4 cup almonds, smoked or toasted, chopped
* 1-2 tsp flax seeds
* 3 Tbsp canola oil or coconut oil
* 1 Tbsp honey
* 1/2 tsp cinnamon
* 1/2 tsp cardamom (optional)
* 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
* salt to taste
* 1/4-1/3 cup dried cranberries
* 1/4 cup shredded coconut (optional)
* 3/4 cup chocolate chips

# Pre-heat oven to 350F/175C.
# In a large mixing bowl, combine oats, almonds, and flax seeds.
# Mix oil, honey, cinnamon, cardamom, vanilla extract, and salt in a small bowl, then drizzle over oat mix.
# Bake in a large pan for 10 minutes. Add cranberries and stir if needed. Bake an additional 5-10 minutes.
# If using coconut, add a few minutes before removal.
# Return granola to mixing bowl and slowly add chocolate while stirring. Allow heat to melt the chocolate while avoiding clumping.
# Once cooled to room temperature, store in an airtight container. Refrigerate for extended freshness.
