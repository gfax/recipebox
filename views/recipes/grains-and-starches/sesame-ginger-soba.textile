h2. Sesame Ginger Soba

* 1/3 cup tahini
* 1/3 cup water
* 2 Tbsp rice vinegar
* 1 Tbsp fresh ginger, minced
* 1 Tbsp miso paste
* 2 tsp soy sauce
* 1 tsp sesame oil
* 8 oz soba noodles, cooked and rinsed
* 1 large cucumber, julienned
* 4 green onions, chopped
* 1 handful fresh cilantro, chopped
* 2 Tbsp black sesame seeds, toasted

# In a bowl, whisk together tahini, water, vinegar, ginger, miso, soy sauce, and oil.
# Toss in noodles, cucumbers, green onions, cilantro, and sesame seeds. Serve room temperature or chilled.
