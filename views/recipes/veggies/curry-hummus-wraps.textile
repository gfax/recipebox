h2. Curry Hummus Wraps

* 1 pack of multi-grain-flax or whole-wheat bread wraps
*Hummus spread:*
* 1 (15 oz) can chickpeas, rised and drained
* 1/2 small lemon, juiced
* 1/2 tsp mint leaves (optional)
* 1 Tbsp tahini
*Sauce:*
* 1 half of a (14 oz) can of coconut milk
* 1/4 cup tahini
* 1 & 1/2 Tbsp green curry paste
* 1 & 1/2 Tbsp soy sauce
* 2 Tbsp brown sugar
*Veggies:*
* 1 Tbsp coconut or canola oil
* 1 Tbsp sesame seed
* 1 tsp ginger root, minced
* 2 small carrots, cut into matchsticks
* 2 cups broccoli
* 1/2 bell pepper, sliced long-ways
* 3 cloves garlic, minced
* 3 green onions, sliced
* 3 cups spinach leaf
* 1 handful cilantro, rinsed and chopped

# Add chickpeas, lemon juice, tahini, and mint to processor and pur√©e.
# In a small pot, add sauce ingredients. Heat and mix until all ingredients are fully integrated. Set aside until ready to use.
# In a large pan, add oil, sesame seed, ginger root, and carrots. Cook until fragrant.
# Add broccoli, bell pepper, garlic, and onions. Cook until everything is tender.
# Add spinach and cilantro. Remove from heat once spinach begins to wilt.
# Spread hummus mix on bread wraps. Add vegetable mix, then drizzle sauce evenly across. Roll up wraps and serve.
