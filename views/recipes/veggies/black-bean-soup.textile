h2. Black Bean Soup

* 1 Tbsp olive oil
* 1 celery stalk, chopped
* 1 red bell pepper, chopped
* 1 large onion, chopped
* 15 oz corn
* 4 cloves garlic, chopped
* 2 Tbsp smoked chile powder
* 1 Tbsp ground cumin
* 4 cups vegetable broth
* 30 oz black beans
* 30 oz black beans, pureé
* 15 oz crushed tomatoes
* 1/2 lime, juiced
* 1/4 cup cilantro
* black peppercorn

# In a large pot, add oil, celery, and pepper. Cook until tender.
# Add onion, corn, garlic, paprika, cumin. Sauté until fragrant.
# Add broth, beans, tomato. Bring to boil.
# Remove from heat. Add lime juice and cilantro. Pepper to taste.
