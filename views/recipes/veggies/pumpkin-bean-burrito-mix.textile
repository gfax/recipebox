h2. Pumpkin Bean Burrito Mix
* 1/4 cup vegetable oil
* 2 small onions, chopped
* 2 tsp minced garlic
* 1 red bell pepper, chopped
* 2 tsp cumin
* 2 tsp chipotle chile powder
* 1/2 jalapeño pepper, seeded, minced
* 2 (15 oz) cans of black beans, rinsed, drained, partially mashed
* 1 tsp oregano (optional)
* 1 & 1/2 cup pumpkin pureé
* 1/2 of a (15 oz) can of pinto beans, rinsed, drained, partially mashed
* 1 tsp salt
* 1 handful freshly chopped cilantro

# Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onions and garlic. Sauté until tender.
# Add bell pepper, jalapeño pepper, cumin and chipotle chile powder. Cook until fragrant.
# Pour beans into skillet and stir.
# Mix pumpkin pureé and salt together in a bowl. Add to skillet and mix.
# Optionally, add oregano. Add pinto beans. Allow the mix to reduce.
# Reduce heat and mix in cilantro.

Best served in a burrito or folded tortilla with queso fresco. Top it with some "salsa":sauce/salsa, enchilada salsa, fresh guacamole, or "pesto":http://www.closetcooking.com/2012/02/swiss-chard-pesto.html.
