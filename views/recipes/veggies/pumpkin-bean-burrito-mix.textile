h2. Pumpkin Bean Burrito Mix

* 1/4 cup vegetable oil
* 2 small onions, chopped
* 2 tsp minced garlic
* 1 red bell pepper, chopped
* 1 jalapeño pepper, seeded, minced
* 1 small chipotle pepper, minced, or 1 tsp chipotle chile powder
* 2 tsp cumin
* 30 oz black beans, rinsed, drained, partially mashed
* 7 oz pink beans or pinto beans, rinsed, drained, partially mashed
* 1 tsp oregano
* 15 oz (about 1 & 1/2 cup) pumpkin pureé
* 1/2 tsp (smoked) salt
* 1 handful fresh cilantro, chopped

# Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onions and garlic. Sauté until tender.
# Add bell pepper, jalapeño pepper, chipotle chile and cumin. Cook until bell pepper is tender.
# Add beans to skillet and stir in oregano.
# Mix pumpkin pureé and salt together in a bowl. Add to skillet and mix.
# Reduce heat and mix in cilantro.

Best served in a burrito or folded tortilla with queso fresco. Top it with some "salsa":sauce/salsa, enchilada salsa, fresh guacamole, or "pesto":http://www.closetcooking.com/2012/02/swiss-chard-pesto.html.
