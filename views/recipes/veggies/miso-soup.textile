h2. Miso Soup

* 1 clove garlic
* 15 oz soy bean sprouts, rinsed and drained
* 4 cups water
* 3 Tbsp miso paste
* 3 green onions, sliced
* 8 oz tofu, diced
* 2 sheets seaweed, shredded

# In a pot, add garlic and sprouts and cook until fragrant.
# Add water and bring to a simmer. Whisk in the miso paste.
# Add onions, tofu, and seaweed. Simmer for 10 minutes.
