h2. Bean Recado

* 1/2 Tbsp olive oil
* 1 onion, chopped
* 1/2 red bell pepper, diced
* 2 jalape√±o peppers, de-seeded and minced
* 3 cloves garlic, minced
* 1 chipotle chile in adobo sauce, minced
* 2 Tbsp ground annatto
* 1 tsp cumin seed
* 1 tsp black peppercorn
* 1 tsp cloves
* 1/4 teaspoon allspice
* 30 oz pink beans, rinsed and drained
* 1/2 cup water
* 1/2 cup mango juice
* 1/2 Tbsp agave nectar or honey
* 1/2 Tbsp molasses or brown sugar
* 3 Tbsp tomato paste
* 1 Tbsp marjoram
* 1 teaspoon (smoked) salt
* 1/2 lime, juiced


# In a large pot on medium heat, add oil and onion and cook until tender. Add peppers and garlic and cook until peppers are tender.
# Add in chipotle chile, annatto, cumin, peppercorn, cloves, allspice and cook until fragrant.
# Add in beans, water, mango juice, agave nectar, and molasses, tomato paste, marjoram, stirring constantly.
# Turn heat to low and cook for 1 & 1/2 hours, stirring every 15 minutes or so and adding water as necessary.
# Remove from the heat when the mixture thickens to a chili consistency. Add lime juice before serving.
