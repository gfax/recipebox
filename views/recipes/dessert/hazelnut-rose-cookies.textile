h2. Hazelnut Rose Cookies

* 1 cup butter, softened
* 2 Tbsp rose water
* 1 cup brown sugar
* 3/4 cup sugar
* 1 & 1/2 tsp vanilla
* 2 eggs
* 1 1/2 cup brown rice flour
* 1 cup hazelnut flour
* 2 tsp xanthan gum
* 1 tsp baking soda
* 1/2 tsp salt
* ~3/4-1 cup hazelnuts, toasted and chopped

# Preheat oven to 375F/190C. In a mixing bowl, combine flours, gum, baking soda, and salt.
# In a separate bowl, combine butter, rose water, sugar and vanilla. Add in eggs and mix until well integrated.
# While stirring the wet ingredients, pour in dry ingredients. Add in hazelnuts and mix until everything is integrated.
# On an ungreased baking sheet, dollop and flatten batter to desirable size. Bake for 8 minutes, or until cookies become firm but not browned.
# Allow cookies to cool for a few minutes before removing from sheet.
