* 1 cup + 2 Tbsp butter, softened
* 2 Tbsp rose water
* 1 cup brown sugar
* 3/4 cup sugar
* 1 tsp vanilla
* 2 eggs
* 2 cups brown rice flour
* 1/2 cup hazelnut flour
* 2 tsp xanthan gum
* 1 tsp baking soda
* 1/2 tsp salt
* ~3/4 cup hazelnuts, toasted and chopped

# Preheat oven to 375F/190C. In a mixing bowl, combine butter, rose water, sugar and vanilla. Add in eggs and mix until well integrated.
# In a separate mixing bowl, combine flours, gum, baking soda, and salt.
# While stirring the wet ingredients, pour in dry ingredients. Add in hazelnuts and mix until everything is integrated.
# On an ungreased baking sheet, dollop batter and flatten. Bake for ~10 minutes, or until cookies become firm but not browned.
# Allow cookies to cool and firm before removing from sheet.
