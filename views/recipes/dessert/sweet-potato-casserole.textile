h2. Sweet Potato Casserole

* 1 cup brown sugar
* 1/3 cup flour
* 1 cup chopped pecans
* 1/4 cups butter, melted and cooled
* 3 cups mashed sweet potato
* 1 cup sugar
* 1/2 tsp salt
* 1 tsp van
* 2 eggs, well beaten
* 1/2 cup butter, melted (1/2 cup)

# Combine brown sugar, flour, nuts, & butter in mixing bowl. Set aside.
# Preheat oven to 350F/175C.
# Combine sweet potato, sugar, salt, vanilla, eggs, and butter in bowl. Mix thoroughly.
# Pour mixture into buttered baking dish.
# Sprinkle the surface of the sweet potato mixture evenly with the crust mixture.
# Bake for 30 minutes. Allow to set 30 minutes before serving.
