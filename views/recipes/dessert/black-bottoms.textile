h2. Black Bottoms

*Topping:*
* 1 (8 oz) package cream cheese, softened
* 1 egg
* 1/3 cup sugar
* 1/8 tsp salt
* 1 cup miniature semisweet chocolate chips
*Mix:*
* 1/2 cup oat flour
* 1/2 cup corn starch or potato starch
* 1/4 cup almond flour or hazelnut flour
* 1/2 tsp xanthan gum
* 1 cup white sugar
* 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
* 1 tsp baking soda
* 1/2 tsp salt
* 1 cup water
* 1/4 cup + 1 Tbsp vegetable oil
* 1 Tbsp cider vinegar
* 1 tsp vanilla extract

# Preheat oven to 350F/175C. Line cupcake pans with paper cups or spray with non-stick cooking spray.
# In a medium bowl, beat the cream cheese, egg, 1/3 cup sugar and 1/8 tsp salt until light and fluffy. Stir in the chocolate chips and set aside.
# In a large bowl, mix together the flour, starch, gum, sugar, cocoa, baking soda and salt. Add water, oil, vinegar and vanilla. Stir together until well blended.
# Evenly distribute the batter mix between muffin tins. Dallop topping in the center of each cake. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes.
