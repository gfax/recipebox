* 1/2 cup olive oil
* 2 large eggs
* 1 cup sugar
* 1/2 cup orange (about 2 oranges), and zest
* 2 tsp orange blossom water
* 3/4 cup corn flour
* 1/2 cup corn starch
* 1/2 cup oat flour
* 3/4 tsp xanthan gum
* 2 tsp baking powder
* 1 tsp salt
* 1 Tbsp sugar

# Preheat oven to 375F/190C. In a mixing bowl, combine oil, eggs and cup of sugar. Add orange juice, zest, and water. Combine until smooth.
# Add flours, gum, baking powder, and salt. Mix until integrated.
# Transfer batter to a greased pan. Sprinkle remaining sugar over cake.
# Bake for 25 minutes, or until an inserted knife comes out clean. Allow to cool 15 minutes before removing from pan.
