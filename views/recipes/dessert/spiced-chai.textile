* 3 cups water
* 3-4 inch cinnamon stick
* 1 tsp whole black peppercorn
* 1 tsp fresh ginger, grated
* 1/2 tsp cardamom seed, removed from pods
* 1/4 tsp nutmeg
* 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
* 1/8 tsp cloves
* 1/2 oz loose leaf black tea
* 1/4 cup brown sugar
* 1 cup whole milk

# Bring water to a simmer and add all the spices. Brew for 10 minutes.
# Add tea and continue brewing for another 3-5 minutes, making sure to not allow tea to over-brew and turn bitter.
# Add brown sugar and stir until dissolved. Remove from heat.
# Stir in milk then pour the liquid through a mesh strainer into a serving vessel.
