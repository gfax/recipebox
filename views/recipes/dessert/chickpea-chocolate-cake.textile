* 15 oz chickpeas, rinsed and drained
* 3 eggs
* 1 cup & 2 Tbsp semi-sweet chocolate chips
* 1/2 cup sugar
* 1/2 tsp baking powder

# Preheat oven to 350F/175C. Mix chickpeas, eggs and 1 cup of chocolate chips in a food processor until smooth. Add sugar, baking powder, and mix until integrated.
# Pour mixture into a greased cake pan and sprinkle in remaining chocolate. Bake for 20 minutes, or until a knife inserted into the cake comes out clean.
