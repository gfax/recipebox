h2. Bourbon Brownies

* 1 cup brown sugar
* 1/2 cup almond flour
* 1/4 cup sorghum flour
* 1/2 tsp salt
* 1/2 tsp baking soda
* 1/2 cup coconut oil
* 5 oz dark chocolate
* 2 eggs, beaten
* 1/2 Tbsp bourbon
* 1/2 Tbsp vanilla

# Preheat oven to 350F/175C. Add sugar, flour, salt, and baking soda to a mixing bowl.
# In a bowl or saucepan, melt and stir coconut oil and chocolate together.
# Add chocolate mix, eggs, bourbon, and vanilla to the mixing bowl and mix until batter becomes thick.
# Pour into a well-greased baking dish and bake for 33 minutes.
