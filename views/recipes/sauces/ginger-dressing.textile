* 1/3 cup rice vinegar
* 1/2 cup peanut oil
* 1/2 onion
* 1 small carrot
* 1 small celery stalk
* 1 clove garlic, minced
* 1/2 lemon, juiced
* 4 tsp soy sauce
* 1 Tbsp mayonnaise
* 1 Tbsp sugar
* 1/2 Tbsp ginger, minced
* 1/2 tsp salt
* 1/4 tsp ground black peppercorn

# Blend ingredients in a food processor until smooth. Chill before serving.
