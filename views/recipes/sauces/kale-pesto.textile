h2. Kale Pesto

* 4 cups kale, stems removed and coarsely chopped
* 1 handful cilantro, coarsely chopped
* 1 jalapeno, coarsely chopped
* 2 cloves garlic
* 1/4 cup pepitas, toasted
* 1/3 cup olive oil
* 2 Tbsp lime juice (~1 lime)
* salt to taste

Puree everything in a food processor.
