h2. Kale Pesto

* 4 cups kale, stems removed and coarsely chopped
* 1 handful cilantro, coarsely chopped
* 1 jalapeno, coarsely chopped
* 2 cloves garlic
* 1/4 cup pepitas, toasted
* 1/3 cup olive oil
* 2 Tbsp lime juice (~1 lime)
* salt to taste

Puree everything in a food processor.

h3. Nutrition Facts

Serving size: 1 cup
Calories 480, Fat 44g (Saturated 6g, Trans 0), Cholesterol 0,
Sodium 62mg, Carbs 18g (Fiber 4g, Sugars 0.5g), Protein 9g
