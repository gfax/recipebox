h2. Tuscan Vinaigrette

* 3/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
* 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
* 3 cloves garlic, minced
* 2 Tbsp fresh oregano, chopped, or 2 tsp dried oregano.
* 1 tsp Dijon mustard
* salt and black peppercorn to taste

# Combine ingredients in a glass jar. Shake well before serving.
